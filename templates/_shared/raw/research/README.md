# raw/research/

Use this folder for public or private research references after they have been reviewed for sensitivity.

## What Could Go Here

- Articles.
- Reports.
- Papers.
- Interview notes.
- Market scans.

## What To Decide Before Adding Files

- Is the source public, internal, restricted, or secret?
- Do you have rights to store and process it?
- Should the source stay local-only instead of committed?
- What future question should the wiki source page answer?

## Public Template Rule

Do not add real research files to this public template. Keep only instructions and placeholders here.

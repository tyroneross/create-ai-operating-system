# Taxonomy

## Page Types

- `source`: summary of one source or source bundle.
- `concept`: durable idea, framework, claim, model, or comparison.
- `work`: project, meeting, decision, customer, operation, or execution record.
- `life`: personal goal, routine, learning plan, trip, household/admin, or reflection.
- `template`: reusable page structure.
- `output`: answer, draft, report, or generated artifact.

## Raw Domains

- `raw/ingest`: drop zone for unprocessed sources.
- `raw/research`: articles, reports, papers, and reference material.
- `raw/engineering`: technical documentation and build notes.
- `raw/other`: material that does not fit another shared folder.
- Variant-specific raw folders are defined in the selected template.

## Status

- `seedling`: draft.
- `sprout`: reviewed.
- `evergreen`: canonical.
- `superseded`: replaced but retained.

## Sensitivity

- `public`: can be shared.
- `internal`: private workspace context.
- `restricted`: human review required before sharing.
- `secret`: never tracked or published.

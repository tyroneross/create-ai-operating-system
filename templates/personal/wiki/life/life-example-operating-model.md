# Example Personal Operating Model

## Bottom Line

This page shows how to keep personal goals and routines in a reusable operating format without exposing private details.

## Current Focus

- Build a weekly review habit.
- Keep the system lightweight.
- Preserve decisions and lessons for reuse.

## Rules

- Capture first, organize second.
- Review weekly.
- Promote only durable lessons into the wiki.

## Review Questions

- What changed this week?
- What decision needs continuity?
- What source or experience should be filed?
- What can be removed or simplified?
